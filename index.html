Come discover Singapore's tech community

Tech is thriving in Singapore! For one afternoon, we've gathered some of the best and most exciting names in our local tech community, all in one place. Come see what they do, hear their stories, and ask them all the questions you've been meaning to ask! Whether you're an anxious student, a fresh graduate, or simply someone with zero tech-experience just curious about what's out there - Tech Fest is for everyone!

Find out what it's like to work in tech and see the cool things you can be a part of

We've got everyone from global tech giants, to home grown companies, to up-and-coming startups. Come ask them what life in tech is like, check out awesome demos, and play with the latest tech products! Discover all the cool things people are doing and find out how you too, can be a part of that.

Hear firsthand experiences from engineers, startup founders, product designers, and more
